# American Financial Group (american-financial-group)
American Financial Group (AFG) is a holding company engaged primarily in property and casualty insurance, focusing on specialized commercial products for businesses, and in the sale of fixed and indexed annuities through its Great American Insurance Group and Great American Life subsidiaries. AFG serves businesses across more than 40 specialty markets including agriculture, aviation, cyber, environmental, equine, ocean marine, professional liability, and transportation.
